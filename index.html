Brow Lamination
Brow Lamination

Brow lamination is a new, innovative beauty treatment that has continued to grow in popularity, recreating the trending look of thick, bushy brows. Brow lamination is great for those who love a ‘brushed-up' look but can’t seem to tame their brow hair due to it being too straight or fine.The brow lamination process itself doesn’t take very long; only around 15-20 minutes. Some brow lamination treatments also include the tinting and/or waxing of brows before or after, which altogether can take around 30-40 minutes in a salon.

Laser Removal Hair
Laser Removal Hair

Laser hair removal is a painless method of permanent hair removal that uses highly concentrated beams of light to destroy hair follicles, preventing their regrowth. Advanced laser technology provides results that will make you feel confident in your skin every day. Whether you want to remove hair from your legs, underarms, bikini area, or any other part of your body, we have a solution for you. The treatments are quick, efficient, and completely safe.

Lash Extension
Lash Extension

Eyelash extensions are individual, semi-permanent false lashes that are attached to your natural eyelashes (one false lash per individual lash). They create dramatically fuller, longer, darker lashes in one 45-minute appointment. To adhere the false lashes to your natural lashes, a semi-permanent, eye-safe glue is used, which will ware off over the course of a 4-8 week period (or more quickly if you used oil-based products or are a rough sleeper).
